About VIORRA

            

            
            

                
Why VIORRA exists:

                
Students rely on generic chatbots like ChatGPT or Claude to review their admissions essays. This is a massive mistake. Commercial AIs are programmed to coddle you. They scrape generic SEO test-prep blogs and tell you your essay is "great" just because it has good grammar. But in Ivy League admissions, being "good" gets you rejected. There is currently no AI on the market designed to tell students the harsh reality they actually need to hear to get accepted.

                
That is why we built VIORRA. VIORRA doesn't guess, and it doesn't sugarcoat. It leverages advanced Chain-of-Thought reasoning against a proprietary database of verified Ivy League essays. It is the only software purpose-built to analyze your essay objectively and deliver honest, actionable feedback.
